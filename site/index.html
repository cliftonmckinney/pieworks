Recruiting is the most important thing startups don't have the time to do. It's hard work, but doing it well is absolutely necessary.

We can help.

Our process starts by taking a look at yours. We analyze your current recruiting strategy, compare it to competitors and best industry practices, and help you make it better.

2) We Help You Build a Proactive Recruiting Process

Our goal is to successfully transition teams from reactive to proactive recruiters. We'll help you build your proactive recruiting process from the ground up.

3) We Manage Your Proactive Recruiting Process For You

Once your proactive strategy is in place, we'll manage it for you. You're busy, and our goal is to help you recruit effectively without being heavily involved.

What is Proactive Recruiting?

Proactive recruiting is a comprehensive, deliberate recruiting strategy that works year-round, whether a company is currently hiring or not.

The goal of proactive recruiting is to build a virtual bench--a group of folks who are interested, engaged, and who fit the company culture perfectly--so when it's time to hire the only tough decision will be which great candidate to choose.
